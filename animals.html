Animal Portfolio

On this site you will find a collection of the coolest animals in my opinion


Animal #1 - the Axolotl

The Axolotl is a walking fish that lives under water in mexico. it looks like a baby for its whole life. It has pink feathery gills on the sides of its head. if it loses a leg or tail, it can regrow them!


Animal #2 - The Platypus

The Platypus is an animal that has a bill that looks like that of a duck! its tail looks like a beavers tail. And its fur is like an otters. even though it is a mammal, it lays egs and has venomous stingers on its feet


Animal #3 - Chameleon

A Chameleon is a liazard and are super good at disguising. they change their skin color to hide from predators or talk to their companions. they have super long tounges and eyes that let them look in more than 1 direciton at once.


Animal #4 - Mantis Shrimp

A manthis shrimp is a normal shrimp but a bit different. it can punch so fast and powerfully that it can shatter the glass in an aquarium. it also sees the most colors than any animal in the whole world.


Animal #5 - Tardigrade

A Tardigrade is a animal that is super tiny. it can survive extreme conditions. such as extreme heat and even on the moon! they are called water bears. they are basically impossible to kill! they are smaller than a grain of salt but can survive boiling water, freezing ice, and even without any breathing air.


Animal #6 - Narwhal

a narwhal is a animal known as the unicorn of the sea. it is a whale with a giant tooth that looks like a horn. it lives in the freezing arctic waters. their long tusks help them to feel the water around them and hunt for fish!
